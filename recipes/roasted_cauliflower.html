Roasted Cauliflower

Description

A healthy and delicious snack that requires almost no ingredients at all.


Ingredients

  • 1 medium/large cauliflower head
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Chop couliflower florets.
  3. Place florets into bowl and drizzle with olive oil. Add a few shakes of salt.
  4. Lay florets on a baking sheet and place in the oven.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes flipping once.
  6. Begin snacking!
