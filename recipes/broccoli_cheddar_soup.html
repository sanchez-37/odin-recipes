Broccoli Chedar Soup (Panera Style)

Description

A copycat recipe of Panera Bread's famous broccoli cheddar soup. It will have a vegetable

base that is just as good and enough to feeed multiple people without costing an arm and a leg


Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups of chopped broccoli florets
  • 1 cup of chopped carrots (in circles)
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 cups of vegetable stock
  • 2.5 cups of sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup melted butter + 1 additional tablesppon
  • 1/4 cup of flour
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Chop broccoli into florets. Chop carrots and onions.
  2. Melt 1 tablespoon of butter. Saute onions until they become translucent.
  3. Whisk 1/4 cup of melted butter and flour together
  4. Gradually pour milk and whisk. Stir vegetable stock into mixture. Bring to a simmer (20 minutes)
  5. Add broccoli, carrots, and sauteed onion. Simmer for about 20 minutes.
  6. Stir cheddar cheese until it melts. Season with salt and pepper.
